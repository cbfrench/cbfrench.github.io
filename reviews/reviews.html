Reviews

Games

  • Never review a game you have not played
  • Try to only review games that you have completed
  • Review different game genres each time to keep your objectivity

Ordering

  • Always begin with objective facts, then end with opinions
  • Begin with presentation, as that's the first thing people see
  • Make sure to mention seemingly pointless details, like music and menu design, as well as the important things like controls and gameplay
  • End on gameplay, and devote most of the review to it unless something else deserves more mention

Scale

  • Use a unique scale if you want to appear consistent
  • Using a decimal-base scale is the most pleasing, but can lead to score padding and angry fans
