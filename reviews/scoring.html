Scoring

As many reviewers do, I've attached a numerical number to the end of my review. I do my best to try to be as objective as possible, but at the end of the day, the review is all about my opinion. Below is my opinion of how games should be ranked, as I find the current method of giving every game above a 7 not beneficial to anyone. I've balanced the ranking below and you can check to see the qualifications for each ranking tier. As I'm trying to simplify things, I will not use decimals in my reviews, so what you see is what you get.

10 - Masterpiece

A game that is outstanding in nearly every area. It is not perfect, as nothing ever is, but the enjoyment gained from it and the sheer craftsmanship of the game itself make it impossible to be ignored and should be played by everyone.

9 - Amazing

A game that creates a huge amount of enjoyment, but has one or two things that are just important enough to hold it back from being a masterpiece.

8 - Great

A game that is a lot of fun, but may be inconsistent or have several minor flaws or a major flaw that can be glossed over because of the enjoyment.

7 - Good

A game that is fun to play and may have many minor flaws that don't detract from the gameplay too much.

6 - Above Average

A game that is somewhat fun to play, even if it has some large flaws.

5 - Average

A game that is the middle of the road. It isn't fun, it isn't bad, it's just alright.

4 - Below Average

A game that is kind of a drag to play. It may have several flaws that make it annoying, or it may just be the nature of the game's design.

3 - Poor

A game that is not fun to play and can actually irritate the player with its flaws or gameplay mechanics.

2 - Flawed

A game with either terrible gameplay or several major flaws that cannot be ignored.

1 - Awful

A game that is truly bad in every definition of the word, with either aggravating gameplay or major flaws across the board.

0 - Unplayable

A game that cannot be played due to its flaws. It is either extremely terrible or cannot be played as intended due to gamebreaking bugs.
